About us

A safe, welcoming start for your child

The Crayon Box opened in 2025 in Latlurcan, Co. Monaghan. It’s a modern, purpose-built setting where we look after babies, toddlers, preschoolers and school-aged children.

Each room is set up for the age group that uses it, so the space feels right whether a child is having a nap, learning their first words or finishing homework. What we want is simple: somewhere local families can trust, where their child feels happy and settled every day.

Our services

Childcare for every age and every family

Whatever stage your child is at, we have a place to suit. Here’s what we offer families in and around Monaghan.

Baby & Toddler Care

1 to 3 years

Calm, attentive care for our youngest children, with a quiet sleep space for naps and lots of one-to-one warmth as they settle in and reach their early milestones.

Preschool & ECCE

3 to 5 years

Play-based preschool rooms where children make friends and build the confidence they’ll take to school. We offer places under the free ECCE pre-school programme.

After-School Care

5 to 12 years

Somewhere relaxed and supervised for after the school bell: a snack, a hand with homework, and time to unwind. It gives working parents cover they can count on.

Summer Camps

5 to 12 years

Holiday camps that keep school-age children busy and happy, with games, crafts and lots of time outdoors.

Full-Day Care

Flexible hours

A full day of care for families who need longer hours, following a steady routine of play, meals, rest and learning in one safe place.

Part-Time Care

Flexible days

Part-time places for families who only need a few sessions or days a week, arranged to fit around your routine.

Places fill up by room and age group, so get in touch to check what’s currently available.
