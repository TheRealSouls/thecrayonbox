About us

A safe, nurturing place to begin the learning journey

The Crayon Box is a modern, purpose-built childcare facility that opened in 2025 in Latlurcan, Co. Monaghan. We provide a warm, safe and stimulating environment for babies, toddlers, preschool children and school-aged children.

Every room has been thoughtfully designed for the age group it cares for — bright, calm and full of opportunities to explore, play and grow. Our goal is simple: to give local families a place they can trust, where children feel happy, settled and supported every day.

Bright, age-appropriate rooms for every stage from 0–12 years

Indoor and outdoor spaces that encourage curiosity and play

A friendly, family-focused approach rooted in the local community

Our services

Childcare for every age and every family

From your baby’s first steps to busy school days and summer adventures, we offer flexible options to fit family life in Monaghan.

Baby & Toddler Care

0–3 years

Gentle, attentive care in calm baby and toddler rooms — with a dedicated sleep space and plenty of warmth as little ones settle, play and reach early milestones.

Preschool & ECCE

3–5 years

Play-based preschool rooms that build confidence, friendships and early learning skills, with places available under the free ECCE pre-school programme.

After-School Care

5–12 years

A relaxed, supervised space after the school day for snacks, homework, play and downtime — giving working parents reliable, friendly cover.

Summer Camps

5–12 years

Summer camps filled with activities, games, crafts and outdoor play to keep school-age children busy and happy through the holidays.

Full-Day Care

Flexible hours

Full-day childcare for families who need longer cover, with a consistent daily routine of care, play, rest and learning in one safe place.

Part-Time Care

Flexible days

Part-time places for families who need a few sessions or days each week — a flexible option that fits around your schedule.

Availability varies by room and age group. Get in touch to check current places.

Financial support

We accept the National Childcare Scheme (NCS)

The Crayon Box accepts the National Childcare Scheme, helping eligible families reduce the cost of childcare. Parents can contact us to discuss availability and how NCS support may apply to your family.
